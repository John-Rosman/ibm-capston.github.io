IBM Capstone

by John Rosman IV

This page was created to hold the contents of my project for the IBM data analytics professional certification. In this case study, I performed many real-world tasks of a junior data analyst.

I undertook the task of collecting data from multiple sources, performing exploratory data analysis, data wrangling and preparation, statistical analysis and mining the data, creating charts and plots to visualize data, and building an interactive dashboard. The project involved working with a variety of data sources, project scenarios, and data analysis tools, including; Excel, SQL, Python, Jupyter Notebooks, and Cognos Analytics.This project culminates with a presentation of my data analysis report, with an executive summary for the various stakeholders in the ficticious case study organization.
